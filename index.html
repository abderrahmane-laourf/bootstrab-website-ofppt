STA Tan-Tan (Institut Spécialisé de Technologie Appliquée de Tan-Tan) is a public vocational training institution in Tan-Tan, Morocco. It's part of the OFPPT network and provides practical, job-oriented training in various technical fields to equip students with the skills needed for successful careers in the region's growing industries.
